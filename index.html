About

Hey! I'm Vic, an enthusiastic Front End developer. HTML, CSS, SVG and Javascript are the raw materials I use in manipulating the fabric of the web. I love working on the web and making sure it continues to be a place for everyone and our creative, personal, and weird passions.

I come from a B2B SaaS Business Development background. During the pandemic and ensuing lockdowns I developed a passion for coding, and began learning the magic of conjuring up websites from nothing. I recently received a Techdegree from Treehouse in Front End Web Development, and now I'm eager to continue expanding my skill set.

I'm currently focused on enhancing my expertise in React & NextJS frameworks, REST API, Express, Node.js, and SQL. These technologies enable me to create seamless, dynamic, and interactive user experiences.

The projects that I have done are meant to showcase the grasp that I have of the technologies that make up the web. My talents and passions are my guide, as I train for Front End Developer life in the next chapter of my career.
